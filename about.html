Hey There!

I have worked on many projects with several developers and studios with my building, terrain designing, project management, and quality assurance testing.
What I Do
Terrain Designing
I have over 3 years of experience with Terrain Designing, and is my best skill! I can create terrain from realistic, to cartoon. I also work with custom PBR textures.
Building
With over 2 years of experience of Studio Building, I most often build in low and high poly. I create all my assets in studio, but sometimes use assets created in Blender.
Quality Assurance
I've been doing QA Testing for a little more than 1.5 years. I've worked with several notable games and studios including Beluga, Roblox DevRel, Base Battles, Cursed Islands, RB Battles, and much more!
My Working Process
This is my general workflow for any projects initiated.
Details & Ideas
We discuss theme, materials, size, layout and more. Examples will need to be provided for the most accurate product.
Map Outline
I will make a quick rough layout of the map, and send it to you for your approval.
Designing
Once I get the green light to proceed, I begin designing the commission.
Final Product
I present the final product to you. Small complementary edits are provided if necessary.
